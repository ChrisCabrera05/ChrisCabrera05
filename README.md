## Who I Am

I'm a data-driven IT professional with **15+ years in the FinTech and payments industry**, currently working as an IT Systems Data Analyst where I manage large-scale SQL database environments, own QA and compliance workflows, and drive API integration work for a global money transfer platform.

By day, I keep payment systems clean, compliant, and running at scale. On the side, I'm building **Lexicore AI** — an AI consulting company helping small businesses in the Inland Empire and Los Angeles area cut through the AI hype and actually implement tools that work.

I've grown through every layer of this industry — from QA Analyst to QA Lead to Systems Data Analyst — and that full-stack operational lens is what makes my work different. I don't just analyze data; I understand the systems, the compliance constraints, and the people that data touches.

## What I Bring to the Table

**FinTech & Compliance**
Deep hands-on experience with AML, KYC, OFAC, and PEP compliance workflows inside live remittance and payments platforms. I understand how regulatory requirements translate into data validation rules, and I build QA processes that hold up under audit.

**SQL & Database Systems**
I live in SQL. Complex query optimization, schema analysis, cross-system data reconciliation, and maintaining integrity across high-volume transactional databases — this is my daily work, not a resume line.

**API Integration & QA**
End-to-end API testing, payload validation, integration workflow design, and data quality assurance across interconnected systems. I've mapped transaction lifecycles at the field level and built the documentation to prove it.

**Applied AI**
I work with LLMs practically — building Copilot Studio agents, designing prompt frameworks, and integrating AI into real business workflows. My **ACHIEVE Framework** is a structured methodology I developed to help organizations move from AI curiosity to measurable value.
